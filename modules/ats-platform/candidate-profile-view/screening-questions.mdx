## Screening Questions

The Screening Questions tab displays the candidate's responses to the initial questions set during the job requisition process.

## Viewing Responses

- Navigate to the Screening Questions tab.
- Scroll through the list of questions and the candidate's corresponding answers.

## Types of Questions

Screening questions can vary and may include:
- Yes/No questions
- Multiple choice questions
- Short answer questions

## Analyzing Responses

When reviewing screening question responses:
- Look for alignment with job requirements
- Consider the overall pattern of responses, not just individual answers
- Use responses to guide further interview questions or assessments

<Tip>
  Use screening question responses as a starting point for further exploration during interviews or follow-up communications.
</Tip>
