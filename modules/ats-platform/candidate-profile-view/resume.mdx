## Candidate Resume

The Resume tab allows you to view, download, and update candidate resumes.

## Viewing the Resume

1. Navigate to the Resume tab in the Candidate Profile View.
2. The resume will be displayed in a preview pane.

## Downloading the Resume

1. Look for the download icon next to the resume file name.
2. Click the icon to download the resume to your device.

## Updating the Resume

1. Click the edit icon (pencil) next to the resume file name.
2. Choose the new resume file from your computer.
3. Upload the new file to replace the existing resume.

<Info>
  The resume preview may include additional information such as a talent summary and candidate digest with communication scores.
</Info>
