## Logging into MOPID

This guide will walk you through the process of logging into the MOPID Applicant Tracking System (ATS).

## Accessing the Login Page

1. Visit [start.mopid.me](https://start.mopid.me)
2. You'll see a login screen with the MOPID logo and "Welcome Back!" message

## Logging In

1. Enter your email address in the "Email" field
2. Enter your password in the "Password" field
3. Click the "Login" button

<Info>
  Your login credentials (email and password) should have been shared with you via email.
</Info>

## Alternative Login Method

You can also use the "Sign in with Google" option if your account is linked to a Google account.

## Forgot Password?

If you've forgotten your password:

1. Click on the "Reset" link below the login button
2. Enter your registered email address
3. An OTP (One-Time Password) will be sent to your email
4. Enter the OTP to verify your identity
5. You'll then be able to set a new password

<Tip>
  Make sure to check your spam or junk folder if you don't receive the OTP email in your inbox.
</Tip>

After successfully logging in, you'll be directed to the [Dashboard](/modules/ats-platform/login-dashboard/dashboard).
