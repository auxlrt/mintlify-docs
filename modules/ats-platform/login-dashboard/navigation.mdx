## Navigating MOPID with Ease

Efficient navigation is key to making the most of MOPID. Let's explore how you can move through the platform smoothly:

## Your Navigation Hub

The left sidebar serves as your navigation hub:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Dashboard Icon">
    Your home base. Click this to return to the main dashboard view from anywhere in MOPID.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Candidates Icon">
    Access your entire talent pool across all jobs. Perfect for a broader view of your candidates.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Moving Between Jobs and Candidates

Here's how you can navigate between different views:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Explore a Specific Job">
    Click on a job title in the Job Summary Table to view its detailed hiring pipeline.
  </Step>
  <Step title="View Candidates by Stage">
    Click on any number in the pipeline stages to see candidates at that specific stage across all jobs.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Access Custom Stages">
    Click the "Custom" column, then select any custom stage to view candidates within it.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Staying Informed at a Glance

Keep an eye on the Jobs Summary on the right side of your dashboard. This quick overview tells you:

- Total number of jobs
- Number of actively published jobs
- Number of closed jobs

<Tip>
  Regularly check the Jobs Summary to stay updated on your overall hiring status without having to dig through details.
</Tip>

## Filtering Your View

Use the Hiring Pipeline dropdown at the top of the dashboard to filter jobs:

1. Click on the dropdown (default is "Open Jobs")
2. Select from:
   - All Jobs
   - Open Jobs
   - Jobs Under Review
   - Closed Jobs

This allows you to focus on the specific type of jobs you need to work on at any given time.

Remember, MOPID is designed to be intuitive and user-friendly. Don't be afraid to explore - the more familiar you become with navigating MOPID, the more efficiently you'll be able to manage your hiring process.
