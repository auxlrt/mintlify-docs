## Exploring Your MOPID Dashboard

Your MOPID dashboard is designed to give you a bird's-eye view of your entire hiring process. Let's dive into its key features:

## Understanding Your Hiring Pipeline

The Job Summary Table is the heart of your dashboard. Here's what you can do:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="View All Jobs">
    See all your jobs at a glance, along with their progress through various hiring stages.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Filter Jobs">
    Use the Hiring Pipeline dropdown to focus on specific job types:
    - All Jobs
    - Open Jobs
    - Jobs Under Review
    - Closed Jobs
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Explore Hiring Stages">
    Each column represents a stage in your hiring process:
    - Sourced
    - Screening
    - Custom stages (click to expand)
    - Offered
    - Offer Accepted
    - No Hire/Reject
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Diving Deeper into Jobs

To get more detailed information about specific jobs or candidates:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Job Details">
    Click on a job title to view its detailed candidate pipeline.
  </Step>
  <Step title="View Stage-Specific Candidates">
    Click on any number in the Job Summary Table to see candidates at that particular stage across all jobs.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Customizing Your Hiring Stages

MOPID respects that every company's hiring process is unique. That's why we've included a "Custom" column in your Job Summary Table.

<Info>
  Click on the "Custom" column to reveal any user-defined stages you've set up for your hiring process. Whether it's "Interview 1" or "HR Round", your custom stages are just a click away.
</Info>

## Keeping Track of Your Progress

On the right side of your dashboard, you'll find a quick summary of your jobs. This Jobs Summary tells you:

- How many total jobs you have
- How many are published (active)
- How many are closed

It's a simple yet effective way to gauge your overall hiring activity at any given moment.

Remember, your dashboard is more than just a display - it's an interactive tool designed to make your hiring process smoother and more efficient. Explore, click around, and discover how MOPID can transform your recruitment efforts.
