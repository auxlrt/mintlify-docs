## Interacting with Candidates

This feature allows you to engage with candidates directly from the Pipeline View, streamlining communication and reducing the need to switch between different tools or platforms.

## Benefits

- Save time by initiating communications without leaving the ATS
- Maintain a record of all interactions within the candidate's profile
- Ensure consistent and timely communication with all candidates

## Available Actions

From the Pipeline View, you can:

1. Review candidate profiles
2. Initiate WhatsApp conversations
3. Initiate phone calls

## How to Interact

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select a Candidate">
    Click on a candidate's card in the Pipeline View to open their profile.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose an Action">
    In the candidate's profile, you'll see options to review their information, send a WhatsApp message, or initiate a call.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Execute the Action">
    Click on the desired action button. The system will either display the candidate's full profile, open a WhatsApp chat window, or initiate a call depending on your selection.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Record the Interaction">
    After the interaction, make sure to log any important notes or outcomes in the candidate's profile for future reference.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Ensure that you have the necessary permissions and contact information before initiating direct communication with a candidate.
</Warning>
