## Searching and Filtering Candidates

These features allow you to quickly find specific candidates or groups of candidates based on various criteria, making it easy to focus on the most relevant applicants for your needs.

## Benefits

- Save time by quickly locating specific candidates or groups of candidates
- Improve hiring efficiency by focusing on the most qualified candidates
- Easily compare candidates based on specific criteria

## Basic Search

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Search Bar">
    Locate the search bar at the top of the Candidate List.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Keywords">
    Type in relevant keywords (e.g., "React Developer").
  </Step>
  <Step title="Initiate Search">
    Press Enter or click the search button to see results.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Use quotation marks for exact phrase matches, e.g., "project manager".
</Tip>

## Advanced Filtering

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Filter Menu">
    Click on the "Filter" button near the search bar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Criteria">
    Adjust the following filters as needed:
    - Experience range
    - Current CTC range
    - Expected CTC range
    - Notice period range
    - Skills
    - Candidate sources
  </Step>
  <Step title="Apply Filters">
    Click "Show Results" to apply the selected filters.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Reset Filters">
    Use the "Reset" button to clear all applied filters.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Available Filters

| Filter | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| Experience | Set minimum and maximum years of experience |
| Current CTC | Define salary range for current compensation |
| Expected CTC | Set range for desired compensation |
| Notice Period | Filter by candidate's availability to start |
| Skills | Select specific skills required for the position |
| Source | Filter by how the candidate entered your ATS |

<Info>
  Filters can be combined to create highly specific candidate lists. Experiment with different combinations to find the most effective approach for your needs.
</Info>

## Saving Searches

To save time on frequently used searches:

1. Perform your desired search or apply filters
2. Click "Save Search" at the top of the results
3. Name your saved search for easy reference
4. Access saved searches from the "Saved Searches" dropdown
