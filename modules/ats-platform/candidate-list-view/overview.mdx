## Candidate Pipeline View

The Candidate Pipeline View is a crucial feature of our Applicant Tracking System (ATS) that allows hiring managers and recruiters to efficiently manage and track job candidates through various stages of the hiring process.

## Key Features

- Visual representation of your hiring funnel
- Easy candidate stage management
- Quick access to candidate information and resumes
- Powerful search and filtering capabilities
- Integrated communication tools
- Assessment initiation and tracking
- Bulk candidate import and export
